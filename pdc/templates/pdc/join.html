    Requirements to Join

    Membership in the Petersburg Democratic Committee is open to any resident of Petersburg who is registered to vote, supports the principles of the Democratic Party, pays the annual dues, and signs and complies with the Declaration Form.

    Process to Join

    Step 1: Sign Declaration Form

    To apply for membership, fill out the Declaration Form and email it to petersburgdemocrats@gmail.com.

    Download Form

    Step 2: Pay Dues

    Dues are $20 per year, and are due on January 1st. You have several options to pay dues:

    1. Electronic Payment: To submit your dues electronically, please use the Zelle app.
      1. Search for petersburgdemocrats@gmail.com to find the payee
      2. Add your name and "{% now "Y" %} Membership dues" in the memo field.
    2. By Check: If you wish you may mail or hand-deliver a check to the Treasurer. Please contact petersburgdemocrats@gmail.com for address and instructions.
