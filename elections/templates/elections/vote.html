    Voter Information Portal

    The Virginia Department of Elections has an online voter information portal where you can:

    • Check whether you are registered to vote
    • Register to vote
    • Apply to vote absentee by mail
    • Find your polling place

    Important Election Dates

    Keep an eye on our calendar for upcoming important dates and deadlines related to elections. If you need to register to vote or apply for absentee voting by mail, be sure to do so as soon as possible. Election day is too late!
