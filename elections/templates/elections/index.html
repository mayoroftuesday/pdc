    November 7, 2023 General Election

    In 2023 the Virgina State Senate and House of Delegates are up for election.

    For full details and lists of candidates, see the Virginia Board of Elections website.

    Positions

    The following positions are up for election in Petersburg:

    • Senate of VA - 13th District
    • House of Delegates - 82nd District

    Learn more about the candidates here.

    Important Dates

    • September 22 - First day of in-person early voting
    • October 16 - Deadline to register to vote
    • October 27 - Deadline to apply for a ballot to be mailed to you
    • November 4 - Last day of in-person early voting
    • November 7 - Primary election
